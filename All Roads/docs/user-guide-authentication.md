## Creating an Account

There are three ways to create a Muster account:

### Option 1: Sign Up with Email

1. Open the Muster app
2. Tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen
3. Fill in your information:
   - First Name (at least 2 characters)
   - Last Name (at least 2 characters)
   - Email Address
   - Username (at least 3 characters, letters/numbers/underscores/dashes only)
   - Password (see password requirements below)
   - Confirm Password
4. Check the box to agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
5. Tap "Register"
6. You're in! Welcome to Muster 🎉

**Password Requirements:**
- At least 8 characters long
- Contains at least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- Contains at least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- Contains at least one number (0-9)
- Contains at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

### Option 2: Sign Up with Apple

1. Open the Muster app
2. Tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen
3. Tap "Sign in with Apple"
4. Follow Apple's authentication prompts
5. Choose a username for your Muster account
6. Tap "Register"
7. You're in! Welcome to Muster 🎉

**Note**: Apple may hide your email address using their Private Relay feature. This is completely secure and works seamlessly with Muster.

### Option 3: Sign Up with Google

1. Open the Muster app
2. Tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen
3. Tap "Sign in with Google"
4. Select your Google account
5. Choose a username for your Muster account
6. Tap "Register"
7. You're in! Welcome to Muster 🎉

## Signing In

### Sign In with Email or Username

1. Open the Muster app
2. Enter your email address or username
3. Enter your password
4. (Optional) Check "Remember Me" to stay signed in for 30 days
5. Tap "Sign In"

**Tip**: If you check "Remember Me", you won't need to sign in again for 30 days. Without it, you'll stay signed in for 7 days.

### Sign In with Apple

1. Open the Muster app
2. Tap "Sign in with Apple"
3. Follow Apple's authentication prompts
4. You're in!

### Sign In with Google

1. Open the Muster app
2. Tap "Sign in with Google"
3. Select your Google account
4. You're in!

## Resetting Your Password

Forgot your password? No problem!

### Request a Password Reset

1. On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter your email address
3. Tap "Send Reset Link"
4. Check your email inbox for a password reset link
5. The link expires after 1 hour for security

### Complete the Password Reset

1. Open the password reset email
2. Tap the reset link (or copy and paste it into your browser)
3. Enter your new password
4. Confirm your new password
5. Tap "Reset Password"
6. Return to the app and sign in with your new password

**Security Tip**: Choose a strong, unique password that you don't use for other accounts.

## Linking Accounts

You can link multiple sign-in methods to your account for convenience.

### Link Apple or Google to an Existing Account

If you already have a Muster account with email/password and want to add Apple or Google Sign In:

1. Try to sign in with Apple or Google
2. If your email matches an existing account, you'll see a prompt: "An account with this email already exists. Would you like to link your Apple/Google account?"
3. Tap "Link Account"
4. Enter your current password to verify your identity
5. Tap "Confirm"
6. Your accounts are now linked!

**Benefits of Linking Accounts:**
- Sign in faster with Apple or Google
- No need to remember your password
- Keep all your data in one account

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid email or password"

**Possible causes:**
- Incorrect email/username or password
- Caps Lock is on
- Extra spaces in email/username

**Solutions:**
- Double-check your email/username and password
- Try resetting your password
- Make sure Caps Lock is off

### "This email is already registered"

**Cause**: An account with this email already exists.

**Solutions:**
- Try signing in instead of signing up
- Use the "Forgot Password?" link if you don't remember your password
- If you're trying to sign up with Apple/Google, follow the account linking prompts

### "Too many login attempts"

**Cause**: You've exceeded the maximum number of login attempts (5 attempts in 15 minutes).

**Solution**: Wait 15 minutes and try again. This security measure protects your account from unauthorized access.

### "No internet connection"

**Cause**: Your device isn't connected to the internet.

**Solutions:**
- Check your Wi-Fi or cellular connection
- Try switching between Wi-Fi and cellular data
- Move to an area with better signal

### "Sign in with Apple/Google failed"

**Possible causes:**
- You cancelled the sign-in process
- Network connection issue
- Apple/Google service temporarily unavailable

**Solutions:**
- Try again
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure you're signed in to your Apple ID or Google account on your device

### Password reset link expired

**Cause**: Password reset links expire after 1 hour for security.

**Solution**: Request a new password reset link from the sign-in screen.

## FAQ

### Q: Is my data secure?

**A**: Yes! We take security seriously:
- All connections use HTTPS encryption
- Passwords are securely hashed (never stored in plain text)
- Tokens are stored in secure, encrypted storage
- We follow industry best practices for authentication

### Q: Can I change my username?

**A**: Currently, usernames cannot be changed after account creation. Choose carefully!

### Q: Can I change my email address?

**A**: Email address changes are not currently supported through the app. Contact support@muster.app for assistance.

### Q: What happens if I forget my username?

**A**: You can sign in with your email address instead. If you need help, contact support@muster.app.

### Q: Can I have multiple accounts?

**A**: Each email address can only be associated with one account. However, you can link multiple sign-in methods (email, Apple, Google) to the same account.

### Q: How long do I stay signed in?

**A**: 
- Without "Remember Me": 7 days
- With "Remember Me": 30 days
- You can sign out manually at any time

### Q: What if I don't receive the password reset email?

**A**: 
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Wait a few minutes (emails can be delayed)
3. Make sure you entered the correct email address
4. Try requesting another reset link
5. If still no email, contact support@muster.app

### Q: Can I use the same password for multiple accounts?

**A**: While technically possible, we strongly recommend using unique passwords for each online account for better security.

### Q: What are the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy?

**A**: You can read our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy by tapping the links on the registration screen or visiting:
- Terms of Service: https://muster.app/terms
- Privacy Policy: https://muster.app/privacy

### Q: How do I delete my account?

**A**: Account deletion is available in the app settings under "Account" → "Delete Account". This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
